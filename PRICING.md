### Billing & Tax Policy 

| Policy | Details | 
| :--- | :--- | 
| **Currency** | All prices are listed in USD. | 
| **Taxes** | Prices are exclusive of VAT/Sales Tax. Total tax is calculated dynamically at checkout based on billing location. | 
| **EU Businesses (non-Italian)** | 0% VAT for non-Italian EU businesses with a valid VAT ID (Reverse Charge). | 
| **Italian Entities** | 22% IVA applies to all domestic transactions. Following the standard for international digital service providers, we do not issue electronic invoices via SdI; Italian B2B customers are charged 22% VAT which they can reclaim through their standard tax filings. | 
| **International** | No VAT for customers in the US, Canada, or outside the European Union. | 
| **Card Country ≠ Residence** | If your card issuing country differs from your billing country of residence, an additional VAT invoice may be issued post-checkout to settle any difference required under EU VAT rules (especially for European residents). | 
| **VAT Adjustments** | If a discrepancy or failed VIES validation requires a VAT correction, we will issue a supplementary invoice. You agree to pay any resulting VAT difference. | 
| **The "Intent" Standard** | Meaningful activity tracking vs. incidental clicks. Capacity resets every 24 hours at 00:00 UTC. | 

> **Italian tax notice (Agenzia delle Entrate)**  
> *I clienti italiani con Partita IVA accettano che l'IVA sia addebitata al 22% senza fattura elettronica SdI, recuperandola autonomamente in dichiarazione. In caso di discrepanza tra paese di residenza e paese della carta di pagamento, o indisponibilità temporanea del sistema VIES, emetteremo fattura integrativa per correggere l'IVA. Procedendo con l'ordine, accetti queste condizioni.*
